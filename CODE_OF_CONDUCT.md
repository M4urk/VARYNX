# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We pledge to make participation in the VARYNX project a harassment‑free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards
Examples of positive behavior:
- Respectful communication
- Constructive feedback
- Collaboration and support
- Focus on improving the project

Unacceptable behavior includes:
- Harassment or discrimination
- Personal attacks
- Trolling or insults
- Sharing private information

## Enforcement
Project maintainers may take action including:
- Warning
- Temporary or permanent ban
- Removal of contributions

## Contact
If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact:
**varynx.security@proton.me**

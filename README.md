## What is VARYNX?

VARYNX is a **100% offline** security suite that protects your Android device without sending data to the cloud. Your privacy is protected by design—no telemetry, no analytics, no tracking.

### Why VARYNX?

| Traditional Security Apps | VARYNX |
|---------------------------|--------|
| ❌ Require constant internet | ✅ Works completely offline |
| ❌ Upload your data to cloud | ✅ All data stays on device |
| ❌ Track your behavior | ✅ Zero analytics or telemetry |
| ❌ Sell aggregated data | ✅ We don't collect anything |

## FAQ

<details>
<summary><strong>Is VARYNX really 100% offline?</strong></summary>

Yes. VARYNX makes zero network requests. You can verify this with any network monitor. The app requests no internet permission.
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>How does it detect threats without cloud?</strong></summary>

VARYNX uses on-device heuristics and pattern matching. All detection logic runs locally on your phone.
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Can I export my data?</strong></summary>

Yes. Go to Settings → Export Data. This creates a local file you control.
</details>
